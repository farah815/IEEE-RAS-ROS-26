Opinion:
The contest was interesting and helpful for improving problem-solving skills and understanding basic programming concepts.
It included a good mix of easy and medium problems that tested input handling, loops, and logic building.

Cons

A few statements were long and slightly confusing at first reading.

suggestions for improvments:
